The hidden problem

Commerce begins with products. People do not.

Customers

  • Cannot find exactly what they need
  • Compromise on design or functionality
  • Contact multiple manufacturers
  • Repeatedly explain requirements
  • Wait days for quotations

Manufacturers

  • Receive vague enquiries
  • Spend hours clarifying requirements
  • Educate customers on materials and constraints
  • Prepare quotations that never convert
The real bottleneck is not manufacturing. It is translating human intent into something manufacturers can actually build.
